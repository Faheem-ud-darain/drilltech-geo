## 7. Fleet & Machine Specifications

### 7.1 Mobile Drill B57

**Hydraulic Feed System**
- Pulldown force: 20,000 lbs (9,072 kg)
- Retract force: 30,000 lbs (13,608 kg)
- Feed speed (down): 62 ft/min (18.8 m/min)
- Feed speed (retract): 41 ft/min (12.4 m/min)
- Rapid: 80 ft/min (24.3 m/min)
- Feed stroke: 6 ft (1.83 m)

**Power Unit**
- Engine: Deutz Tier 3 Diesel, 116 hp (86.5 kW)
- Includes: 12V starting circuit, electronic engine module in operator's control panel, 18-gallon (68 L) lockable fuel tank, lockable battery box, cold weather starting package

**SPT Automatic Hammer**
- Standard: ASTM D-1586
- Available slugs: 140 lb (63.5 kg), 170 lb (77.1 kg), 300 lb (136.1 kg), 340 lb (154.2 kg) — standard and heavy duty

**Right Angle Drive**
- Two-speed heavy duty, tapered roller bearings, ring gear & pinion
- 2 in (50.8 mm) hardened output shaft with flex chain coupling to 1-3/4 in (44.5 mm) kelly bar

**Drill Head Rotary**
- Totally enclosed "dry box" with tapered roller bearings
- 1-3/4 in (44.5 mm) heat-treated alloy steel kelly drive bar
- 3-1/4 in (82.6 mm) ID hollow spindle

**Operating Capacities**
- Hollow stem augers, 4-1/4 in (108 mm) ID: 250 ft (76 m)
- Continuous flight auger, 6 in (152.4 mm) OD: 300 ft (91 m)
- Rotary drilling, NWJ/NW drill rod with 5 in (127 mm) OD bit: 1,500 ft (457 m)

**Hoists**
- *Main:* Single line capacity 8,500 lbs (3,855.5 kg); line speed 0–75 fpm (22.9 m/min); cable diameter 1/2 in (12.7 mm); cable length 100 ft (30.5 m)
- *Auxiliary:* Single line capacity 3,000 lbs (1,361 kg); line speed 0–126 ft/min (38 m/min); cable diameter 5/16 in (8.3 mm); cable length 75 ft (22.9 m)
- *Safe-T Driver:* Single line 900 lbs (variable up, freefall down); cable 1/4 in, length 300 ft
